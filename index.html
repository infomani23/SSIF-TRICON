About us

Discover Our Story

Know more

Triocon Space Frame Technologies (TSF) is an integrated custom design and
manufacturing company specializing in the design, development, production, and
installation of aesthetic 3D space frame structures. We utilize our “advanced bolted
solid ball joint hollow tube technology” in steel, stainless steel, and aluminum. In
addition to space frames, we also design, manufacture, and service other building
forms, miscellaneous steel works, and building materials for the construction industry.

Why Space Frame ?

Space frames provide a lightweight solution to the problem of creating large span enclosures free from obstructions.

Innovation & Work

The conventional steel structures can cross large spans only in one direction but space frames can cross large spans in both directions. This gives great versatility and freedom to the designer in the selection of structural system. Since the self-weight of space frame is small, greater economy is gained in the substructures as an added advantage.

Planning & Implementing

Space frames can be designed in all kinds of forms such as prismatic, cylindrical and spherical in horizontal and vertical geometric shapes. Therefore, it is possible to integrate space frames in the architectural designs of various building types in all industrial and commercial areas.
